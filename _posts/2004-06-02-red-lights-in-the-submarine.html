--- wordpress_id: 21 title: Reflect the project in reality wordpress_url: /?p=21 layout: post --- If you make a change to a project then consider making an accompanying physical change that will help underline the reality of the change.
  • Added a new member to your team? Move them into the same location as everyone else.
  • Promoted someone? Move them somewhere slightly different.
  • Gone into release mode? Shift the staff who will be most busy into a "situation room".
  • Changed process? Restructure your forms/reports/templates (if you have them) or meeting agendas.
Find any change that will help even the most non-communicative and insular nerd notice that something is different.
