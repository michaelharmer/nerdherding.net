--- wordpress_id: 25 title: "\"See how little you can do, and deliver that much as quickly as possible.\"" wordpress_url: /?p=25 layout: post --- Johanna Rothman has an interesting new post about rework - her list of remedies is good and includes this little beauty:
"See how little you can do, and deliver that much as quickly as possible."
For me, that quote almost entirely sums up agile approaches to software development. Do a little, quickly. See where its wrong, re-orient and start again.
